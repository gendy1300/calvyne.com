Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?
Open the App Store on your iPhone → tap your profile picture in the top right → tap Subscriptions → find Vesara → tap Cancel Subscription. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period.
My plan isn't generating. What should I do?
Check your internet connection and try again. If the issue persists, close the app fully and reopen it. Still stuck? Email us at support@calvyne.com and we'll sort it out.
How do I request a refund?
Refunds are handled by Apple directly. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in with your Apple ID, find the charge, and select Request a Refund.
I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
On the login screen, tap Forgot Password and enter your email address. You'll receive a reset link within a few minutes. Check your spam folder if it doesn't arrive.
Can I use Vesara on multiple devices?
Yes. Sign in with the same account on any iOS device and your data will sync automatically.
