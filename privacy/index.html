Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information.

Last Updated: April 2026

Overview

Calvyne ("we", "our", "us") built the Vesara app. This policy explains what information we collect when you use Vesara, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

By using Vesara, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information to provide and improve our service:

  • Account information: email address and password when you create an account.
  • Profile information: your goals, challenges, and preferences that you provide to personalise your experience.
  • Usage data: plans you generate, tasks you create, and interactions with the AI coach.
  • Device information: device type, OS version, and app version for debugging purposes.
  • Purchase information: subscription status verified through Apple. We do not store your payment details.
How We Use Your Information
  • To generate personalised AI plans and coaching responses.
  • To sync your data across your devices.
  • To send push notifications for your scheduled plan steps (only if you allow notifications).
  • To verify your subscription and unlock premium features.
  • To improve the app and fix issues.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

AI Processing

Vesara uses AI to generate plans and coaching replies. Your profile information and inputs are sent to our backend server to produce these responses. We use Google Gemini as our AI provider. Your data is processed in accordance with Google's data processing terms.

We do not use your personal data to train AI models.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS) and secure password storage. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — database and authentication.
  • Google Gemini — AI plan and coaching generation.
  • Apple App Store — subscription and payment processing.

Each service has its own privacy policy governing how they handle data.

Children's Privacy

Vesara is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where required by law.
